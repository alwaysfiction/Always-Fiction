Books

Disturbing Peace (Book 1)

Purchase on Amazon!

The world has traded individuality for control. To keep society “safe”, The Leaders will monitor every thought and every emotion. Showing too much emotion? You disappear.

Poppy Rivers has always been too loud, too angry, and far too unwilling to conform. After a personal tragedy threatens to unravel her entirely, the MDA marks her as a danger to society and her grief becomes her greatest liability.

But Poppy knows the truth: this isn't really peace, it’s surveillance masked as safety. With her family on the edge of being torn apart, she must decide how far she’s willing to go to protect them…even if it means losing herself.

In a world where freedom of thought is never permitted, staying silent might be the most dangerous choice of all.

Disrupting Chaos (Book 2)

Purchase on Amazon!

Poppy Rivers thought she knew fear—until she wakes up inside the sterile walls of the unknown. She’s now at the mercy of The Leaders’ most ruthless form of control.

But this time, survival means more than just resistance in staying silent. With her family’s fate unknown and a world unraveling around her, Poppy must resist becoming a piece of their distorted game.

As the spark between and her and Cane grows, that light may flicker through the turmoil of this world where love is a weakness…

Secrets long buried will surface. Trust will be shattered. And Poppy will be forced to face a reality that that could ignite a rebellion…or destroy what’s left of her.

About

Welcome to Always Fiction! What started as one author’s dream to inspire creativity has grown into a passionate publishing company dedicated to helping authors achieve their dreams through their stories! We believe in the artistry of storytelling to inspire and connect others as we work closely with emerging and independent authors to bring their characters into the world. Whether you're a reader searching for new creative worlds or a writer ready to share your story, we’re happy you’re here and welcome you to Always Fiction!
